    Four stages of our acknowledge of intelligence:

    • Acting humanly: The Turing Test approach

      • natural language processing
      • knowledge representation
      • automated reasoning
      • machine learning
      • computer vision
      • robotics to manipulate

      Note: the first step of AI is to make them act as human

    • Thinking humanly: The cognitive modeling approach
      There are two approaches: an algorithm performs well on a task and that it is therefore a good model of human performance, or vice versa.
